# Cookie Policy

This policy explains which cookies and similar technologies (like browser local storage) the Service uses, in line with the EU ePrivacy rules. The short version: **strictly necessary cookies to run the site, a couple of optional interface preferences, and no advertising or third-party analytics cookies at all.**

## Strictly necessary cookies

These are required for the Service to function and cannot be switched off. Under ePrivacy they do not require consent.

| Cookie | Purpose | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| `frontendparts_session` | Keeps you signed in and carries your session between pages (cart-free, account, CSRF protection context). | Session / until logout |
| `XSRF-TOKEN` | Cross-site request forgery protection for form submissions. | Session |

## Preference cookies and storage

These remember interface choices. The site works without them — you just get the defaults on each visit.

| Name | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| `sidebar:state` | Cookie | Remembers whether the dashboard sidebar is open or collapsed. |
| `appearance` | Local storage | Your theme choice (light / dark / system) in the app area. |
| `sidebar` | Local storage | Sidebar visibility in the app shell. |
| Preview-layout keys | Local storage | Guest preview-modal pane layout (signed-in users store this on their account instead). |

## What we deliberately do not use

- **No third-party analytics or tracking cookies.** Usage stats are recorded as first-party, server-side events — see the [Privacy Policy](/privacy) — not via cookies or third-party scripts.
- **No advertising, retargeting, or social-media pixels.**
- **No cookie banner dark patterns.** Because only strictly necessary cookies are set, no consent wall blocks the site.

## Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time. Blocking the strictly necessary cookies will sign you out and break login-protected features; blocking preference cookies only resets the interface defaults. Local-storage preferences can be cleared from your browser's site-data settings.

Changes to this policy will be posted here with a new "last updated" date. Questions: [support ticket](/dashboard/tickets/new) or the contact details in the [Legal Notice](/legal-notice).
