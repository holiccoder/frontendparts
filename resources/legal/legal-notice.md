## Merchant of record

Purchases are processed by **Paddle.com** as our merchant of record. This means:

- Paddle — not the operator — appears as the seller on your card statement, receipts, and invoices;
- Paddle collects and remits applicable sales taxes (VAT, GST, sales tax) as seller;
- payment data is handled under Paddle's own buyer terms and privacy policy, presented at checkout.

Your contractual relationship for the Service itself is with the operator named above; your payment transaction is with Paddle.
