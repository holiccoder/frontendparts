# Refund Policy

Every purchase is covered by a **{{ refund_window_days }}-day refund window**, no questions asked. This applies to every plan and every billing period — monthly, quarterly, yearly, and lifetime — and to purchases made through any payment backend we offer (currently Paddle, our merchant of record; domestic wallets such as WeChat Pay and Alipay once domestic payments are available).

## How to request a refund

1. Sign in and open a [billing support ticket](/dashboard/tickets/new?category=billing) from your dashboard.
2. Mention the order you want refunded (your [orders page](/dashboard/orders) lists every purchase) — "please refund my latest order" is enough.
3. We confirm and issue the refund to the original payment method through the payment provider that processed the order.

Refunds go back through the provider that took the payment: Paddle for card/PayPal purchases, or the domestic wallet you paid with when domestic payments are offered. Processing time after we issue the refund depends on the provider and your bank — typically a few business days.

## After the window

Outside the {{ refund_window_days }}-day window, refunds are at our discretion — but if something is genuinely broken, open a ticket and we will make it right. You can always **cancel a subscription** instead: cancellation stops the next renewal and you keep full access until the end of the period you already paid for. Cancelling is not a refund of the current period.

## What happens after a refund

- Your plan access ends when the refund is issued.
- The [License](/license) for the refunded purchase ends with it — the access that purchase granted stops with the refund.
- Anything on the Free plan is unaffected.

## Chargebacks

Please open a ticket before filing a chargeback — refunds through the channel above are faster and guaranteed within the window. Unjustified chargebacks may lead to account suspension while we resolve them with the payment provider.

Contact: [billing ticket](/dashboard/tickets/new?category=billing) · questions about this policy via the [Legal Notice](/legal-notice) contact details.
