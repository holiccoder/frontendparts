# Copyright & Takedown Policy

This page explains how we approach intellectual property on the Service, and how to reach us if you believe anything on it infringes your rights — including exactly what happens after you do, and how fast. Replace this text with your own policy before launch.

## Our content

- Content published on the Service is produced by us or by contributors who confirmed they hold the necessary rights.
- We **never knowingly copy** third-party code, images, text, logos, or trademarks. Sample and placeholder content is original or used under a free license.
- If we ever reference third-party work, we do so with attribution and within the bounds of fair use.

## Requesting a takedown

If you own rights in work that appears on the Service and believe it infringes, we want to hear from you — the channel below reaches the person who can actually remove content.

**The fastest path: open a [takedown ticket](/dashboard/tickets/new?category=takedown)** (category *Takedown* — a free account is all you need). If you cannot or do not wish to create an account, email the operator contact listed in the [Legal Notice](/legal-notice) with the same information.

Please include:

1. **Your work** — what you believe is affected, with URLs where available.
2. **The material** — where on the Service it appears.
3. **Your relationship** — that you own the rights or are authorized to act for the owner.
4. **What you want** — removal, modification, or a change to attribution.
5. **Contact details** so we can reply.

A short, good-faith statement that the use is not authorized is enough — no legalese required. Attachments help but are optional.

## Our response SLA

- **Acknowledgement within 2 business days** of your ticket or email.
- **Substantive response within 5 business days** — removal of the material, an agreed modification, or a reasoned explanation of why we believe the use is lawful.
- Material under active review may be **unpublished while we assess** credible requests.

Repeatedly unfounded or abusive requests may be declined with reasons. Valid requests are never met with legal threats.

## Counter-notifications

If you are a contributor and believe content was removed by mistake, reply on the same ticket thread with your reasoning and we will review it under the same SLA.
