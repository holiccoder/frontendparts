# Affiliate Program Terms

These terms govern participation in the affiliate program for the Service. By joining the program from your [dashboard](/dashboard/affiliate) you accept these terms in addition to the [Terms of Service](/terms). The program lets you earn a commission on qualifying purchases made by buyers you refer through your tracked link.

## Joining and your referral link

Any registered user may join self-serve from the dashboard. On joining you receive a unique referral code and a tracked link (`/r/{code}`). Clicks on your link are recorded (IP address, user agent, landing URL and timestamp) and attributed with a **{{ cookie_days }}-day first-party cookie**, last-click wins. A referred visitor who signs up within that window is linked to you, and purchases they make are attributed to you even if the cookie is later lost, as long as the referral code reaches checkout.

## Disclosure requirement (FTC)

You **must clearly and conspicuously disclose** that you earn a commission whenever you promote the Service — in every blog post, video, social post, newsletter, comparison page or any other content that contains your referral link. Disclosure must be unambiguous and placed close to the link ("I earn a commission if you buy through this link" is fine; a vague "this post may contain affiliate links" buried in a footer is not). This follows the U.S. FTC Endorsement Guides; equivalent rules apply in many other jurisdictions, and you are responsible for complying with the law wherever your audience is.

## Promotional rules

The following are **prohibited** and lead to suspension and voided commissions:

- **Brand-bidding and trademark ads** — no paid advertising that bids on or targets our brand name, our trademarks, misspellings of them, or "brand + modifier" keywords (e.g. "brand coupon"), on any ad network or search engine.
- **Impersonation** — no ads, domains, social accounts or pages that present themselves as official properties of ours, and no framing or mirroring of our site.
- **Spam** — no unsolicited bulk email, comment spam, forum spam or automated posting; email promotions must comply with CAN-SPAM / GDPR / PIPL as applicable and go only to recipients who opted in to hear from you.
- **Cookie-stuffing and forced clicks** — no iframes, pop-ups, redirects, browser extensions or any other technique that drops the tracking cookie without a genuine user click.
- **Self-referral** — purchases made by you, by accounts sharing your email address, or through your own link for your own use never earn commission.
- **Misrepresentation** — no false claims about the Service's features, pricing, discounts or licensing, and no invented coupons or deals.

## Commissions

- You earn **{{ commission_rate }}% of the net amount** (excluding taxes) of every attributed purchase.
- Subscription renewals earn commission for the first **{{ recurring_months }} months** from the original conversion; lifetime plans earn a one-time commission.
- Attribution is **last-click**: the most recent valid referral before checkout wins.
- New commissions start as **pending**. They become **payable** after the {{ refund_window_days }}-day refund window plus a **{{ holding_days }}-day holding period** have elapsed since the sale.

## Payouts

- Commissions are paid out in a **monthly batch** once your payable balance reaches the payout threshold of **${{ payout_threshold }}** (CNY commissions are normalized to USD at the platform's configured FX rate for the threshold check). Balances below the threshold roll over to the next month.
- Payouts are sent manually via **PayPal or Wise** to the payout method saved on your affiliate dashboard; the method on file at batch time is used, so keep it current. We mark the payout paid with the provider's payment reference.
- You are responsible for any taxes on your affiliate earnings and for providing accurate payout details.

## Refunds, chargebacks and clawbacks

If a referred order is **refunded or charged back before payout**, its commission is voided and never paid. If fraud, a terms violation, or an erroneous credit is discovered **after** a commission has been paid, we may **claw it back**: we will deduct the amount from future commissions, or invoice you for it if no future commissions cover it.

## Suspension and termination

We may suspend an affiliate account at our discretion — typically for the prohibited conduct above or suspected fraud. Suspension stops your link from recording clicks and earning new commissions; your history is kept, and commissions already earned are still handled under these terms unless they resulted from a violation, in which case unpaid commissions are voided. You may leave the program at any time by contacting support; legitimately earned payable commissions are still paid out.

## Changes

We may adjust program parameters (commission rate, cookie window, holding period, threshold) from the platform settings; the values shown on this page are always the current ones. Material changes to these terms will be announced by email or in the dashboard before they take effect.
